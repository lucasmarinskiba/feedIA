## The Mission
FeedIA replaces graphic designers for social media content creation. Enable millions of entrepreneurs, small businesses, agencies to create professional carousel/reel/story/post content without hiring designers.

## 3 Pillars INTEGRATED

### 1. INSTAGRAM ALGORITHM INTELLIGENCE (Phase 31)
Signals that matter:
- **Feed:** User activity + post info + person info + interaction history
- **Stories:** View frequency + participation + closeness
- **Explore:** Post popularity + rapid interactions + niche matching
- **Reels:** Watch completion + shares + creator popularity

**FeedIA Action:** Every carousel generated is algorithm-optimized:
- Niche clarity built into copy
- First 3 seconds unmissable (stop-scroll designed)
- Engagement hooks (comments > saves > shares > likes)
- Recency + consistency signals embedded

### 2. GRAPHIC DESIGN 2026 (AI Conscious)
Not replacing designers = Automating design work designers do:
- IA explores visuals (territories, layouts, styles)
- System enforces brand consistency (tokens, components, variables)
- Typography + hierarchy + spacing optimized
- Accessibility built in (contrast, legibility, motion)
- Responsive + interactive outputs (not just static)

**FeedIA Capability:** Create branded carousel templates without hiring:
- Color palettes (Warm Organic, Bold Playful, Dark Premium, Clean Editorial + custom)
- Typography systems (20 font pairings, hierarchies pre-tested)
- Layout patterns (25+ Pinterest-proven compositions)
- Motion guidance (fade, slide, pop, zoom - timed for platform)

### 3. SOCIAL TRENDS 2026 (Strategic + Measurable)
Trends shaping content strategy:
- **Social Search:** Treat captions as SEO (keywords, hashtags, titles matter)
- **Authenticity:** Real person > polished corporate
- **Communities:** Niche audiences > mass reach
- **Performance:** Comments + saves + clicks > impressions + vanity metrics
- **IA + Criterion:** IA accelerates, but brand voice decides

**FeedIA Action:** Every piece:
- Keywords for social search built in
- Authentic voice preserved (IA drafts, brand reviews)
- Community-focused messaging (not mass appeal)
- Metrics-ready (tracks what matters)
