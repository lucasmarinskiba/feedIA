## VISUAL TECHNIQUES EXTRACTED

### Technique 1: Extreme Low-Angle Perspective
```
Low-angle shot looking UP at subject, wide-angle lens distortion
Subject appears towering, dominant, confident
Hands/body in extreme foreground (forced perspective)
Sky or ceiling in background
Creates: confidence, power, achievement feeling
```

### Technique 2: Floating Objects Chaos
```
Multiple objects suspended in mid-air around subject
Papers, coffee, tools, products floating
Subject in center, overwhelmed or controlling chaos
Cinematic lighting, shallow DOF
Creates: busy, chaotic, high-energy feeling
```

### Technique 3: Spark/Particle Effects
```
Glowing sparks or light particles around head/face/body
Warm bokeh lights in background (out of focus)
Subject's face emotional (stressed, focused, determined)
Creates: energy, intensity, emotion amplification
```

### Technique 4: Water Splashes/Motion
```
Water or liquid splashing in foreground
Action captured mid-splash
Subject in confident pose
Wide-angle perspective emphasizing drama
Creates: dynamism, freshness, power
```

### Technique 5: Bright Sky Backdrop
```
Intense bright blue sky filling background
Subject backlit or rim-lit by sun
Sun glare/lens flare for drama
Extreme contrast between subject and sky
Creates: aspirational, professional, premium feeling
```

### Technique 6: Product Integration
```
Product held prominently in foreground
Subject's hands/body framing product
Product in focus, subject slightly blurred
Advertising-style composition
Creates: product desire, prestige
```
