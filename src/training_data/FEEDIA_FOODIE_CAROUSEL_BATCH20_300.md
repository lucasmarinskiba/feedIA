## Batch 20 Carousel Structure Analysis (4 Real Carousels — Foodie Focus)

### Carousel 1: Street Food World Tour (Brown + Yellow Palette)
**Slide Structure:**
- Slide 1: Title hook "STREET FOOD POPULAR AROUND THE WORLD"
- Slides 2-8: Individual food item showcase (Stroopwafels, Banh Mi, TieoOkbokki, Arancini, Takoyaki, Shrimp Fried)
- Slide 9: Interactive poll "CHOOSE WHICH FOOD IS YOUR FAVORITE?"
- Slide 10: CTA "FOLLOW US FOR MORE POSTS"

**Design Pattern:** Circular food photography, bold typography, yellow/brown color scheme, minimal text body, country tags (BELANDA/VIETNAM/KOREA/ITALIA/JEPANG/INDONESIA), logo watermarks per slide.

### Carousel 2: Ice Cream Gelato (Pink + Blue Palette)
**Slide Structure:**
- Slide 1: "SUA MELHOR DESCOBERTA" (Best Discovery)
- Slide 2: "PERFEITO EM TODO MOMENTO" (Perfect Every Moment) — Hand holding product
- Slide 3: "NOVIDADE" (New Product) — Product hero + pricing
- Slide 4: "VERÃO PEDE SORVETE" (Summer Craves Ice Cream) — Person eating
- Slide 5: "PECA JÁ O SEU GELADO DE MORANGO" (Order Your Strawberry Gelato)
- Slide 6: "HOJE É DIA DE PEDIR SEU SORVETE FAVORITO" (Today Order Favorite)
- Slide 7: "FELICIDADE EM CADA MORDIDA" (Happiness Every Bite)
- Slide 8: Flavors matrix/options
- Slide 9: Social proof/testimonials
- Slide 10: Final CTA

**Design Pattern:** Vibrant pink/blue split backgrounds, hands holding product, close-up food detail, playful typography, emoji accents, pricing visible, social media icons.

### Carousel 3: Hamburger Promo (Red/Orange + Cream)
**Slide Structure:**
- Slide 1: "O HAMBURGUER PERFEITO!" (Perfect Burger)
- Slide 2: "LEVE 2 PELO PREÇO DE 1" (Buy 2 Pay 1) — Two burgers stacked
- Slide 3: "COMPRE UM HAMBURGUER" (Buy a Burger) — Single burger hero + combo offer
- Slide 4: "BATEU A FOME A TÔMÉ?" (Getting Hungry?) — Burger section/layers
- Slide 5: "BATATA FRITA CROCANTE" (Crispy Fries) — Fries close-up
- Slide 6: "ONION RING" (Onion Ring) — Circular rings detail
- Slide 7: "QUAL O TAMANHO DA SUA FOME?" (What's Your Appetite?) — Large layered burger
- Slide 8: "O HAMBURGUER DE PADRÃO!" (Standard Burger) — Crown/premium positioning
- Slide 9: Closing CTA

**Design Pattern:** Red/orange/cream gradient backgrounds, explosive typography, product macro shots, calls-to-action every 2-3 slides, sizing options emphasized, promotional messaging bold.

### Carousel 4: Churros Delivery (Brown + Cream + Heart Theme)
**Slide Structure:**
- Slide 1: "CHURROS QUENTINHOS feitos com amor" (Warm Churros Made with Love)
- Slide 2: "CHURROS NUNCA é de mais" (Churros Never Too Much) — Person eating, full mouth joy
- Slide 3: "PEÇA SEU CHURRO SEM SAIR DE CASA DELIVERY" (Order Delivery Without Leaving)
- Slide 4: "ANTOJOS DE ALGO Salado" (Cravings Salty)
- Slide 5: "TEQUEDARE COMPRE CADA BOCADO" (Each Bite Worth) — Product presentation
- Slide 6: "RIQUISIMO" (Delicious) — Multiple churros overhead
- Slide 7: "VOCÊ É O DOCE DE LEITE QUE FALTAVA NO MEU CHURROS" (You're the Dulce de Leche) — Heart shape churro
- Slide 8: "JÁ ESTAMOS ABERTOS" (We're Open) — Location/hours
- Slide 9: "IRREISTÍVEIS E CROCANTES" (Irresistible Crispy) — Hand holding

**Design Pattern:** Warm brown/cream palette, heart shapes integrated design, person interaction (faces, hands), emotional copywriting, location/delivery emphasis, accessibility focus (phone number/hours/address).
