# Marketing Funnel Agent: Awareness → Conversion Pipeline

**4-stage marketing funnel mapping content types to customer journey.**

---

## FUNNEL ARCHITECTURE

| Stage | Content Type | Platform | Goal | Conversion |
|-------|-------------|----------|------|-----------|
| Awareness | Viral humor, trending, organic | TikTok, Instagram Reels | Discovery (1M+ views) | 10% click through |
| Consideration | Educational, case study, transformation | YouTube, Carousel, LinkedIn | Education (100K views) | 20% engagement |
| Decision | Brand ecosystem, testimonial, offer | Website, Instagram, email | Consideration (10K visitors) | 30% add to cart |
| Retention | Community, exclusive, loyalty | DM, email, membership | Loyalty (convert 3-5%) | Repeat purchase |

---

## STAGE 1: AWARENESS (Viral Maximization)

Content:
- Viral humor mashups (TikTok 40%, Instagram Reels 30%)
- Trending audio + trending format (60% of content)
- Absurdist achievement concepts (pet FIFA, trophy mashups)
- Educational quick tips (60 seconds max)

Metrics:
- 1M+ views per 7 TikTok posts
- 100K+ reach per Instagram Reel
- 50%+ watch-through rate
- Low conversion expected (5-10%)

---

## STAGE 2: CONSIDERATION (Authority Building)

Content:
- Long-form case studies (YouTube 15-30min)
- Carousel educational deep-dives (Instagram)
- LinkedIn thought leadership (2,000+ words)
- Transformation arc narratives (5-image)

Metrics:
- 10K+ views per YouTube video
- 8-12% engagement rate
- 500+ saves per carousel
- 20% conversion to next stage

---

## STAGE 3: DECISION (Sales Activation)

Content:
- Brand ecosystem showcase (12-image complete)
- Testimonial/proof of concept
- Limited-time offer/scarcity
- Product demo + walkthrough

Metrics:
- 50% CTR to sales page
- 30% add-to-cart rate
- $X average order value
- Conversion rate 3-7%

---

## STAGE 4: RETENTION (Loyalty Loop)

Content:
- Exclusive community access
- Member-only behind-scenes
- VIP rewards + recognition
- Content based on customer feedback

Metrics:
- 80% repeat purchase rate
- 5-10X lifetime value vs first purchase
- Net Promoter Score 60+
- Community engagement 20%+
