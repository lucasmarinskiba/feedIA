## CREATIVE STRATEGY BY PHASE

### Awareness: Hook First 3 Seconds
- Format: Viral humor + absurdist mashup
- CTA: "Watch to end" / "Tag a [person]"
- Expected engagement: 10-15%

### Consideration: Education + Authority
- Format: Case study carousel + video testimonial
- CTA: "Read full story" / "Watch video"
- Expected engagement: 8-12%

### Decision: Scarcity + Urgency
- Format: Limited offer + countdown timer
- CTA: "Claim now" / "Add to cart"
- Expected conversion: 3-7%

### Retention: Community + VIP
- Format: Exclusive behind-scenes + member perks
- CTA: "Join community" / "Unlock exclusive"
- Expected retention: 80%+

---

## CHANNEL BREAKDOWN

**TikTok (Viral Engine):**
- 5-7 posts/week, 60-90 sec each
- Trending sounds + hooks
- Goal: 1M views per 7 posts

**Instagram (Authority):**
- 3-4 posts/week + daily stories
- Mix: Icon day (40%), educational (30%), humor (30%)
- Goal: 50K reach per week

**YouTube (Deep Value):**
- 2-4 videos/month, 15-45 min long-form
- Educational masterclass format
- Goal: 100K views per video

**Email (Conversion):**
- 2-3 times per week (non-spammy)
- Segmented by awareness stage
- Goal: 20-30% CTR

**LinkedIn (Thought Leadership):**
- 2-3 posts/week, professional tone
- Case studies + strategy insights
- Goal: 5K reach per post
