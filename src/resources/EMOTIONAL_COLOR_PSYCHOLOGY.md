## COMPLETE PALETTE SYSTEM

### GREY (Beginning)
- Hex: #6B8E99 → light
- Mood: Calm, composed, serious
- Pose: Bench sitting, hands clasped
- Expression: Hopeful but focused
- Use: Foundation stories, unknown talent
- Psychology: "I am ready, but unproven"

### CORAL (Struggle)
- Hex: #E8A8A8 → fade
- Mood: Energy, effort, ambition
- Pose: Athletic dynamic, sweat-soaked
- Expression: Determined effort
- Use: Hustle narratives, grind stories
- Psychology: "I am working hard"

### ORANGE (Breakthrough)
- Hex: #FF6600 → fade
- Mood: Intensity, momentum, victory
- Pose: Trophy elevated, explosive energy
- Expression: First triumph emotion
- Use: Victory moments, breakthrough announcements
- Psychology: "I have arrived"

### GOLD (Reign)
- Hex: #FFD700 → shadow
- Mood: Achievement, fulfillment, confidence
- Pose: Double exposure power, silhouette
- Expression: Unstoppable dominance
- Use: Peak positioning, championship reigns
- Psychology: "I am unstoppable"

### PURPLE (Legacy)
- Hex: #8B00FF → shadow
- Mood: Transcendence, immortality, timelessness
- Pose: Icon day commanding presence
- Expression: Serene legendary
- Use: Hall of fame, eternal positioning
- Psychology: "I am eternal"
