```
- Name: [FULL_NAME] or [STAGE_NAME]
- Born: [BIRTH_DATE] or [ORIGIN_DATE]
- Nationality: [COUNTRY]
- Height/Weight: [PHYSICAL_STATS] (if applicable)
- Role: [OFFICIAL_FIFA_DESIGNATION]

Design Specifications:
- Background color: [COUNTRY_FLAG_PRIMARY_COLOR]
- Secondary accent: [COUNTRY_FLAG_SECONDARY_COLOR]
- Geometric elements: [COUNTRY-SPECIFIC_SYMBOLS - flags, stars, crosses, lions, eagles]
- Card frame: Dark bar (dark shade of country color) at bottom with white text
- Branding: Panini logo visible + [COUNTRY_FEDERATION_BADGE] (AFA, CBF, FMF, etc.)
- Watermark: Panini FIFA 2026
- Flag circle: [COUNTRY_FLAG] in corner

Visual Elements:
- Grid overlay or geometric patterns (optional, 2-3px lines, subtle)
- Professional photography quality
- High contrast between subject and background
- Centered composition (subject fills 70% of frame)

Tone & Style:
- Humorous positioning (celebrity in sports role)
- Professional trading card authenticity
- Fan-service and shareable appeal
- Absurdist but respectful

Resolution: 1080x1350px (Instagram vertical optimized)
```

- Italy: Green + White + Red (Renaissance graphics)

**Asia:**
- South Korea: Red + Blue (Yin-yang graphics)
- Japan: Red + White (Sun/Mt. Fuji graphics)
- China: Red + Gold (Dragon graphics)
- India: Orange + White + Green (Ashoka chakra graphics)

### Typography

- **Headline (Card name):** Bold sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter), 28-36px
- **Stats (Bottom bar):** Clean sans-serif (Inter, Roboto), 12-16px
- **Position label:** Bold accent, 18-24px
- **Country/Federation:** Smaller, 10-14px

### Visual Elements

- Flag colors in background (dominant)
- Country-specific geometric patterns (shields, stars, crosses, eagles, etc.)
- Country badge/federation seal (prominent in corner or bottom)
- Number (large, in background)
- Panini watermark (standard placement)
- Professional photography lighting (stadium, serious, authority)
