## LIGHTING SETUPS

### Setup 1: Gold Accent Exposure
```
Primary light: Warm gold accent light (60-70 degrees angle).
Fill light: Soft white light from front (40-50% intensity).
Back light: Subtle rim light defining edges.
Result: Gold accents catching embroidered details, warm premium feel.
```

### Setup 2: Cinematic Sidelighting
```
Primary: Strong sidelighting creating depth and shadow.
Accent: Gold or warm secondary light emphasizing jersey details.
Fill: Minimal fill creating dramatic contrast.
Result: Cinematic depth, dramatic professional aesthetic.
```

### Setup 3: Studio Professional
```
Primary: Even studio lighting from above (70-80 degrees).
Accent: Gold catchlight emphasizing embroidered crests.
Back: Subtle backlight defining subject from background.
Result: Professional studio quality, detail-rich capture.
```
