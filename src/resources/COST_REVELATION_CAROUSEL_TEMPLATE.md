# Cost Revelation 4-Slide Carousel Template

**Complete carousel flow extracted from single hiring cost education example.**

This is a FULLY ADAPTABLE template. Use this structure for any cost/expense/decision comparison scenario.

---

## The 4-Slide Structure

### Slide 1: PRICE SHOCK HOOK
**Purpose:** Stop scroll, activate curiosity + assumption challenge

**Visual:**
- Money stack visual (bills, coins, documents)
- Clean background
- Professional photography

**Copy Formula:**
```
Quanto custa um [PROFESSIONAL/THING] de R$ [AMOUNT] para [CONTEXT/USE CASE]?

[Smaller text, secondary question:]
Você acha que custa [ASSUMED_AMOUNT]?
```

**Psychology:**
- Opens with question (curiosity)
- Challenges assumption ("you think it costs X")
- Creates expectation of revelation (viewers swipe to know real cost)

**Examples by Industry:**

| Industry | Example |
|----------|---------|
| **Hiring** | Quanto custa um profissional CLT de R$ 4.000 para sua clínica? |
| **Marketing** | Quanto custa uma campanha de R$ 5.000 para sua loja? |
| **Fitness** | Quanto custa um coach de R$ 500/mês para seu estúdio? |
| **E-commerce** | Quanto custa um operador de R$ 3.000 para seu e-commerce? |
| **SaaS** | Quanto custa um desenvolvedor de R$ 8.000 para seu MVP? |
| **Real Estate** | Quanto custa uma propriedade de R$ 500k para seu investimento? |

---

### Slide 2: FEAR IDENTIFICATION + MYTH-BUST
**Purpose:** Emotional resonance, identify real problem (not what they think)

**Visual:**
- Dark background + gold/yellow accent
- Typography emphasis (highlight key word in color)
- Elegant, premium feel

**Copy Formula:**
```
O problema não é [SURFACE_ASSUMPTION].

O problema é [REAL_ISSUE]: [EMOTION/BEHAVIOR], [CONSEQUENCE1] e [CONSEQUENCE2].

[Optional: Highlight one key phrase in contrasting color]
```

**Psychology:**
- Negation hook (captures attention)
- Reveals that their understanding is incomplete
- Names emotional root cause (fear, confusion, lack of calculation)
- Creates recognition ("oh, that's exactly me")
- Builds trust (company understands real problem)

**Examples by Industry:**

| Industry | Full Copy |
|----------|-----------|
| **Hiring** | O problema não é escolher CLT. O problema é escolher no medo, sem calcular margem e sem entender risco. |
| **Marketing** | O problema não é gastar R$ 5k. O problema é gastar no escuro, sem estratégia e sem medir resultado. |
| **Fitness** | O problema não é contratar coach. O problema é contratar pela ego, sem avaliar qualidade e sem medir ROI. |
| **E-commerce** | O problema não é contratar operador. O problema é contratar por desesperação, sem vetting e sem documentação. |
| **SaaS** | O problema não é contratar dev. O problema é contratar por preço, sem entender qualidade e sem avaliar manutenção. |

**Key Elements to Highlight in Color:**
- Fear emotion: "no medo", "no escuro", "por desesperação"
- Behavioral root: "escolher", "gastar", "contratar"

---

### Slide 3: STRUCTURED COMPARISON + CONSEQUENCES
**Purpose:** Empower through option clarity, systematize decision

**Visual:**
- Gold/yellow gradient background
- Vertical line divider (left side)
- Clean, organized typography
- Scannable list format

**Copy Formula:**
```
Agora compare com outros modelos:
• [OPTION1]
• [OPTION2]
• [OPTION3]

Cada um tem:
• [CONSEQUENCE1]
• [CONSEQUENCE2]
• [CONSEQUENCE3]
• [CONSEQUENCE4]
```

**Psychology:**
- Shifts from emotion to logic
- Provides decision framework (empowerment)
- Lists tangible differences (education)
- Consequence focus (shows implications, not just features)

**Examples by Industry:**

| Industry | Options | Consequences |
|----------|---------|--------------|
| **Hiring** | CLT, Autônomo (CPF), PJ, Sócio de Serviço | Estrutura diferente, impacto tributário, risco trabalhista, lucratividade |
| **Marketing** | Freelancer, Agência, In-house | Expertise diferente, tempo de resposta, controle, custo escalonável |
| **Fitness** | Grupo, Personal, Virtual | Atenção diferente, custo diferente, flexibilidade, resultados |
| **E-commerce** | Marketplace, Próprio site, Híbrido | Taxa de comissão, controle de marca, investimento inicial, crescimento |
| **Real Estate** | Compra, Aluga, Joint-venture | Capital inicial, fluxo de caixa, risco, crescimento de patrimônio |

---

### Slide 4: CALCULATION REALITY CHECK
**Purpose:** Ground decision in concrete numbers, connect to outcome impact

**Visual:**
- Dark background + gold/yellow number highlights
- Clean typography hierarchy
- Organized breakdown list

**Copy Formula:**
```
Vamos fazer a conta real:

[ITEM]: R$ [AMOUNT]
• [EXPENSE1]
• [EXPENSE2]
• [EXPENSE3]
• [EXPENSE4]
• [EXPENSE5 - optional]

[REALITY_CHECK SENTENCE]: [HIDDEN_COST]. E isso impacta diretamente [OUTCOME_CONSEQUENCE].
```

**Psychology:**
- Moves from abstract to concrete (specific numbers = credibility)
- Reveals hidden costs (expenses beyond initial price)
- Connects to personal consequence (affects YOUR profit margin)
- Actionability (viewers now informed, can decide)

**Examples by Industry:**

| Industry | Calculation | Impact |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| **Hiring CLT** | Salário R$ 4.000 + INSS patronal + FGTS + Férias + 1/3 + 13º + Adicionais = Muito mais | Impacta sua margem |
| **Marketing Campaign** | R$ 5.000 + Agência markup + Adset testing + Revisions + Reporting = Total cost | Impacta seu ROAS |
| **Fitness Coach** | R$ 500/mês + Material + Studio rent allocation + Insurance = Cost per client | Impacta sua lucratividade |
| **E-commerce Operator** | R$ 3.000 + Taxes + Benefits + Training + Turnover cost = Actual annual cost | Impacta sua operação |
| **SaaS Developer** | R$ 8.000 + Onboarding + Project management + Bug fixes + Maintenance = Total investment | Impacta seu time velocity |

## How to Adapt This Template

### Step 1: Choose Your Context
- What are you comparing? (hiring, service, product, investment)
- What's the main decision? (choosing between options)
- What hidden costs exist? (often not obvious to audience)

### Step 2: Fill in the Formula
1. **Slide 1:** What price shock will stop them?
   - Pick a realistic salary/cost
   - Challenge a common assumption
   
2. **Slide 2:** What's the REAL problem?
   - Not the choice itself, but HOW they're making the choice
   - What emotion drives bad decisions? (fear, desperation, ignoring numbers)
   
3. **Slide 3:** What options exist?
   - 3-4 main options
   - 4-5 consequences that differ across options
   
4. **Slide 4:** What's the math?
   - Base cost + hidden expenses
   - Final number (total annual cost, etc)
   - How it impacts THEIR bottom line

### Step 3: Adapt Visuals
- Slide 1: Money visual (stack, documents, growth symbol)
- Slide 2: Dark + gold accent (professional, premium)
- Slide 3: Gold gradient + organized list
- Slide 4: Dark + gold highlights on numbers

### Step 4: Adapt Copy Tone
- Slide 1: Conversational question (curiosity)
- Slide 2: Formal, direct negation (trust-building)
- Slide 3: Clean, informational (clarity)
- Slide 4: Educational, consequence-focused (actionable)

## Retention Mechanics: Why This Works

| Slide | Mechanic | Why It Works |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | **Price shock + assumption challenge** | Stops scroll, creates curiosity gap ("I need to know the real answer") |
| 2 | **Fear naming + emotional recognition** | Viewers think "that's exactly me" → high engagement, share-worthiness |
| 3 | **Structured options + consequences** | Shifts from emotion to logic, empowers decision-making |
| 4 | **Specific numbers + personal impact** | Credibility + relevance (affects THEIR business/life) |

**Why people swipe all 4 slides:**
- Slide 1 → "I'm curious, what's the real cost?"
- Slide 2 → "Wait, is that me? Am I making this mistake?"
- Slide 3 → "OK, so what ARE my options?"
- Slide 4 → "What's the actual total? How does it affect me?"

## When to Use This Template

✓ Hiring/staffing decisions
✓ Service/agency comparisons
✓ Investment decisions (real estate, equipment, tech)
✓ Cost-benefit education
✓ Decision-making process help
✓ Fraud prevention (revealing hidden costs prevents mistakes)

✗ NOT for: Product launches, brand stories, inspiration content

---

## Call-to-Action After Carousel

After Slide 4, viewers are:
- Informed (know real costs)
- Emotionally validated (fear named + normalized)
- Decision-ready (have framework + numbers)

Best CTA:
- "Consulta con nosotros" (personalized guidance)
- "Descobrir seu custo real" (lead magnet: calculator)
- Contact button (direct booking)

NOT: Sales pitch. Viewers already feel educated + understood.

## Metrics to Track

- Slide 1 view rate: Does price shock stop scroll?
- Slide 2 save rate: Is fear naming share-worthy?
- Slide 3 view rate: Do people want the comparison?
- Slide 4 engagement: Do specific numbers drive comments/questions?
- CTA click-through: Are they ready to decide?

If Slide 2 has high saves, fear naming worked.
If Slide 4 has high shares, numbers credibility worked.
If all 4 have high view rates, the narrative flow works.
