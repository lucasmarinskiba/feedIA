# Viral Character/Pet Card Prompts: AI Generation Framework

**Complete prompt system for generating humorous AI-created character cards (Panini/Trading card style).**

Source: FIFA 2026 pet cards (Argentina, Brazil, Mexico, Australia). Adaptable to any event/context/subject.

---

## Master Prompt Template (Midjourney/DALL-E)

```
Create a professional trading card (Panini style) of [SUBJECT] as [CHARACTER_ROLE].

Subject: [SUBJECT_DESCRIPTION]
- Appearance: [VISUAL_DETAILS]
- Expression: [EMOTION]

Context: [EVENT/TEAM/ORGANIZATION]
- Uniform/Branding: [TEAM_JERSEY or OUTFIT]
- Position/Role: [POSITION]
- Number: [JERSEY_NUMBER]

Card Design:
- Background: [COLOR] + geometric shapes/graphics
- Layout: Headshot + mid-torso (50% frame)
- Card frame: Dark bar at bottom for stats
- Stats displayed: Name "[NAME]" | Date [DATE] | Stats [HEIGHT/WEIGHT/ROLE]
- Branding: [ORGANIZATION_LOGO] + flag [COUNTRY_FLAG]
- Watermark: [PANINI or BRAND_NAME]

Tone: Humorous, professional athlete-style, fan-friendly
Style: Photograph-quality, AI-generated character card
Resolution: 1080x1350px (vertical Instagram optimized)
```

## Design Elements to Specify

### Background Colors (by context):
- **FIFA:** Country-flag colors (white-light-blue for Argentina, yellow-green-blue for Brazil)
- **Olympics:** Gradient of Olympic ring colors
- **Corporate:** Brand primary color
- **Entertainment:** Platform brand color (purple for Twitch, red for YouTube)

### Geometric Graphics:
- Curved shapes/pawn pieces (FIFA context)
- Rings/torch graphics (Olympics)
- Controller/esports graphics (gaming)
- Gears/tech graphics (tech products)
- Chef's knife/plate graphics (food)

### Stats Bar (Bottom):
- **Template:** Dark background (black or dark color of brand)
- **Text:** White, clean sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter)
- **Layout:** Name | Date/Year | Role/Stat | Additional metric

### Card Branding:
- Official logo (Panini, FIFA, Olympics, brand name)
- Country/Team badge
- Number/designation
- Watermark positioning (bottom corner)
