# Product Advertising Master Templates

**Core Principle**: Design language control via prompts. Not just generating visuals — understanding design language completely.

**Director-Level Control**:
- Font style (serif/grotesk/editorial/luxury/playful)
- Layout hierarchy (magazine/grid/product-hero/lifestyle)
- Color grading & mood (earthy/minimal/high-fashion/vibrant/premium)
- Ad storytelling direction (campaign/launch/lifestyle/aspirational/emotional)
- Camera perspective (hero/detail/lifestyle/editorial/commercial)
- Composition rules (rule-of-thirds/symmetry/depth/scale)

**Result**: Directing like a creative director, not just generating pixels.
