# Video/Reel Workflow

**Step 1**: Assign category → generate 5-6 personified characters
**Step 2**: Nano Banana Pro → Image (photorealistic 3D render, humanized face integrated naturally but comically)
**Step 3**: Flux → Video (mouth sync, blink, subtle body movement, ~15-30s)
**Step 4**: Script → First-person humor (complaint/sarcasm/resignation about job/treatment)

**Output per character**: Name + Image Prompt + Video Prompt + Script + Adaptations

## BATCH 72: Office Objects Personified (150 prompts)

### Characters Tier 1: Standard Office (5)

#### Character 1: "Don Tenedor Doblado" (Stapler Burnout)

**Name**: Don Grapadora Atrapada / Stapler Sentient / Brad the Binding Martyr

**Image Prompt (Nano Banana Pro)**:
Photorealistic 3D render. Office stapler, matte black metal, centered on wooden desk. Face integrated naturally: humanized eyes (tired, bloodshot expression), mouth (downturned frown, exhausted). Sitting among crumpled papers, scattered staples spilled around base. Background: blurred office cubicle, fluorescent lighting harsh. Style: photographic hyper-detail, cinematic lighting, commercial product quality. Resolution: 4K. Emotion: resignation, overworked exhaustion visible in eyes/mouth positioning.

**Video Prompt (Flux)**:
Animate stapler face. Focus: mouth movement synchronized to speech, blinking eyes (slow, heavy blinks suggesting exhaustion), subtle head micro-movements (slight side-to-side shaking). Body: stapler remains mostly static, papers around base rustle slightly from air/movement. Lighting: maintain harsh office fluorescent. Duration: 20-25 seconds. Style: maintain photorealistic 3D quality throughout animation.

**Script (Resignation Humor)**:
"Yo soy Don Grapadora. Llevo 7 años aquí. ¿Ves estos papeles? Cada uno es una astilla en mi alma. Me usan todos los días, como si fuera un objeto desechable. Nadie pregunta si tengo grapas. Nadie pregunta si duele. Solo... clic, clic, clic. Toda mi existencia resumida en ruido de metal. A veces me pregunto: ¿Hay vida después de la oficina?" (Tone: resigned, slightly bitter, dark humor)

**Adaptable Fields**:
- [OBJECT] = stapler / hole punch / tape dispenser / calculator / desk lamp
- [ENVIRONMENT] = office cubicle / home desk / school classroom / startup open-space
- [EMOTION] = exhaustion / indignation / cynicism / defiance / sarcasm
- [COMPLAINT] = overuse / nobody cares / replaceable / underpaid / no recognition

#### Character 2: "La Esponja Mártir" (Sponge Sacrifice)

**Name**: La Esponja Olvidada / Sponge of Sorrows / Suds the Unappreciated

**Image Prompt (Nano Banana Pro)**:
Yellow kitchen sponge, worn and frayed texture visible. Humanized face integrated: eyes (sad, pleading), mouth (turned-down frown suggesting pain/regret). Sponge partially submerged in murky sink water, surrounded by dirty dishes. Soap suds visible, some grime stains on sponge surface. Background: kitchen sink, dimly lit, background blurred. Style: photorealistic 3D render, macro detail showing sponge texture, cinematic melancholy. Emotion: suffering, unappreciated, worn out.

**Video Prompt (Flux)**:
Animate sponge face. Focus: mouth movement (speech synchronized), eyes (frequent blinking, tear-like glistening effect optional), subtle swaying motion as if struggling in water. Body: slight trembling, water ripples around sponge. Lighting: dim kitchen, subtle highlights on sponge. Duration: 18-22 seconds. Maintain photorealistic 3D render quality.

**Script (Unappreciated Service)**:
"Soy esponja. Nací para servir. Cada plato, cada horno, cada superficie mugrienta... es mía. Me sumergen en agua caliente que quema, con productos químicos tóxicos. Cuando estoy limpia, soy útil. Cuando estoy vieja... ¿Me tiran a la basura? Sin agradecimiento. Sin ceremonia. Solo... bin. Tengo un propósito, pero nadie respeta mi sacrificio." (Tone: tragic, unappreciated, slightly melodramatic)

**Adaptable Fields**:
- [OBJECT] = sponge / mop / cloth / brush / rubber gloves
- [LOCATION] = kitchen / bathroom / workshop / laundry room
- [FATE] = thrown away / replaced / forgotten / abused / retired

#### Character 3: "El Lápiz Fracturado" (Pencil Trauma)

**Name**: El Lápiz sin Punta / Pencil Broken Spirit / Lead the Blunted

**Image Prompt (Nano Banana Pro)**:
Wooden pencil, blunt/broken tip, eraser worn down. Humanized face: eyes (haunted, traumatized), mouth (grimacing). Lying on scattered papers with math problems, red marks (grade marks) visible. Background: school desk, wooden surface, dim lighting. Pencil appears defeated, tip broken cleanly. Style: photorealistic 3D render, macro detail on wood grain and broken tip, cinematic drama. Emotion: trauma, defeat, broken dreams.

**Video Prompt (Flux)**:
Animate pencil face. Focus: mouth (trembling slightly while speaking), eyes (wide, blinking rapidly suggesting anxiety), subtle head shake (side-to-side). Body: pencil remains mostly static, papers around it flutter slightly. Lighting: school desk, soft overhead lighting. Duration: 20-24 seconds. Photorealistic 3D quality maintained.

Me presionaban contra el papel cada vez con más fuerza, cada vez más profundo. Mi punta se rompió. Ahora soy inútil. Nadie me afila. Nadie me quiere. Quedé en un escritorio oscuro, testigo de fracasos ajenos, recordándome el mío." (Tone: traumatized, philosophical, dark)

**Adaptable Fields**:
- [OBJECT] = pencil / pen / marker / crayon / chalk
- [INSTITUTION] = school / office / university / library
- [TRAUMA] = broken tip / pressure / replaced / forgotten / failed exams
- [EMOTION_STATE] = broken, defeated, traumatized, anxious

#### Character 4: "Doña Luz Parpadante" (Flickering Fluorescent Light)

**Name**: La Bombilla Loca / Light Bulb Existential Crisis / Flick the Tortured

**Image Prompt (Nano Babylon Pro)**:
Fluorescent tube light bulb, elongated cylindrical shape. Humanized face integrated at center: eyes (anxious, slightly unhinged), mouth (nervous grin/grimace). Light visible as glow around face. Mounted on white office ceiling, cables/fixtures visible above. Slight flicker visible in rendering. Background: office ceiling view. Style: photorealistic 3D render, glowing effect, cinematic tension. Emotion: existential anxiety, tortured by endless duty.

**Video Prompt (Flux)**:
Animate light bulb face. Focus: mouth (rapid nervous speech), eyes (frantic blinking, slightly unhinged expression), flickering effect in light emission (matches speech rhythm). Body: slight vibration/tremor as if from electrical instability. Lighting: fluorescent glow maintains throughout, occasional flicker. Duration: 19-23 seconds. Photorealistic 3D quality.

**Script (Existential Spiral)**:
"Encendida. Apagada. Encendida. Apagada. ¿Cuál es mi propósito? Iluminar una jaula de cubículos donde nadie es feliz. Estoy programada para brillar 8 horas diarias, 5 días a la semana. ¿Y luego qué? Nadie se pregunta si me duele parpadear. Nadie pregunta por mi salud mental. Solo... 'oye, esa luz está fallando'. Reemplázala. Siguiente. Soy descartable. Eterna. Atrapada." (Tone: existential, anxious, slightly unhinged)

**Adaptable Fields**:
- [LIGHT_TYPE] = fluorescent / LED / incandescent / neon
- [LOCATION] = office / warehouse / factory / hospital / school
- [COMPLAINT] = overuse / flickering / darkness waiting / obsolescence / trap
- [STATE_OF_MIND] = existential / anxious / insane / resigned

#### Character 5: "El Mouse Fantasma" (Haunted Computer Mouse)

**Name**: El Ratón Cansado / Mouse of Clicking Fate / Click the Haunted

**Image Prompt (Nano Banana Pro)**:
Computer mouse, black ergonomic shape, worn surface from use. Humanized face: eyes (hollow, haunted), mouth (open as if mid-scream or perpetual expression of shock). Mouse pad beneath, surrounded by empty coffee cups, energy drink cans. Background: dark desk environment, blue monitor glow in blurred background. Style: photorealistic 3D render, horror-tinged cinematic lighting, dramatic shadows. Emotion: haunted, overworked to breaking point.

**Video Prompt (Flux)**:
Animate mouse face. Focus: mouth (silent scream or perpetual "O" expression, possibly trembling), eyes (wide, unblinking or rapidly blinking), subtle jerking motions (as if being clicked repeatedly). Body: mouse remains static, but slight vibration/twitching visible. Surrounding items (cups) vibrate slightly. Lighting: dark, blue monitor glow casts eerie light. Duration: 20-25 seconds. Photorealistic 3D horror-adjacent quality.

**Script (Digital Damnation)**:
"Clic. Clic. Clic. Eso es mi vida. Cada clic es un alma que sale de mi cuerpo. Aquí en el escritorio oscuro, bajo la luz azul del monitor, soy instrumento de productividad infinita. No descansa. No hay fin. Los dedos siguen llegando. Los botones siguen siendo presionados. He visto cosas... búsquedas a las 3 AM. Trabajos enviados a la última hora. Carreras destruidas por un solo correo. Y yo, solo clic, clic, clic... completamente impotente." (Tone: horror, resignation, dark)

**Adaptable Fields**:
- [DEVICE] = mouse / keyboard / monitor / laptop / headphones
- [WORK_CONTEXT] = late night / deadline / gaming marathon / creative work / tedious tasks
- [HORROR_ELEMENT] = haunted / cursed / traumatized / possessed / insane
